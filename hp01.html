Introduction to the Project

Preserving LGBTQ History in Georgia

Welcome to the LGBTQ History Non-Profit of Georgia. Our mission is to collect, preserve, and share the rich history of the LGBTQ community in Georgia.

Why the LGBT+ Archives Project Matters

The LGBTQ community in Georgia has a vibrant and diverse history. Our archives project aims to capture and preserve the stories, struggles, and triumphs of our community members for future generations.
