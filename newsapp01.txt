== Requirements

*Must have:*
- Real-time news updates from reliable sources.
- User authentication and profiles.
- Customizable news feed based on user preferences.
- Push notifications for breaking news.
- Search functionality for finding specific news articles.
- Offline reading capabilities.
- Support for multimedia content (images, videos).

*Should have:*
- Social sharing options for news articles.
- Bookmarking and saving articles for later reading.
- Categories and tags for better content organization.
- In-app feedback and reporting system.

*Could have:*
- Community forums for discussion.
- Integration with social media platforms.
- Dark mode for user interface.
- Localization for multiple languages.

*Won't have this time:*
- In-depth analytics and user behavior tracking.
- Advanced AI-driven content recommendations.
